## About

SummFlux helps businesses transform WhatsApp conversations into organized sales opportunities.

Instead of manually reviewing chats, teams can automatically:

- Analyze conversations with AI
- Qualify leads
- Identify objections
- Receive next-step recommendations
- Track opportunities through a CRM pipeline
- Generate sales insights

---

- AI Conversation Analysis
- Lead Qualification
- Objection Detection
- Follow-up Recommendations
- CRM Pipeline
